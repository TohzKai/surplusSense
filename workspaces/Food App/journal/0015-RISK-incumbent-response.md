# RISK: Incumbent Competitive Response

**Date**: 2026-04-25
**Type**: RISK
**Slug**: incumbent-response

## Finding

If SurplusSense gains traction, incumbents (Treatsure, Yindii) may respond by adding merchant-side features.

## Risk

Treatsure/Yindii have existing merchant relationships and consumer traffic. They could add:

- Merchant dashboards
- Surplus prediction tools
- Discount recommendation

## Mitigation

**Differentiation Strategy**:

1. **Deeper merchant intelligence**: Not just listing - operating intelligence
2. **Surplus prediction first**: Before consumers even browse
3. **Discount recommendation logic**: Rule-based, explainable
4. **Food-safety gating**: Built-in, not bolted on
5. **Recovery analytics**: Track waste reduction over time
6. **Learning loop**: Merchants improve as they use the product

## Positioning

> "Not another consumer marketplace - upstream operating intelligence"

Treatsure/Yindii optimize consumer discovery. SurplusSense optimizes merchant operations.
