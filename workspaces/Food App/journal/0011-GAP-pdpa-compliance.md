# GAP: PDPA Compliance Requirements

**Date**: 2026-04-25
**Type**: GAP
**Slug**: pdpa-compliance

## Finding

The MVP uses synthetic data. Any real merchant or consumer pilot must comply with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

## Requirements

- Consent for data collection
- Purpose limitation
- Access and correction rights
- Data protection obligations
- Breach notification
