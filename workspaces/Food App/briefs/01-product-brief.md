# Product Brief

## Product

A food waste reduction marketplace that connects food businesses (restaurants, bakeries, grocery stores, cafes) with surplus or near-expiry food to budget-conscious consumers who purchase at discounted prices. The platform uses ML to predict surplus, personalize recommendations, optimize dynamic pricing, and provide waste analytics to merchants — turning food waste from a cost center into a revenue stream.

## Objectives

- Reduce food waste by enabling merchants to sell surplus food at a discount rather than discarding it
- Provide budget-conscious consumers access to quality food at significantly reduced prices
- Use ML models to predict surplus quantities, personalize deal recommendations, optimize pricing, and surface waste patterns
- Explore commercial viability as a startup in Singapore's F&B market
- Deliver a working prototype demonstrating ML capabilities for the SMU MBA Machine Learning individual project

## Tech Stack

- Backend: Python (FastAPI or Flask)
- Frontend: Flutter or React/Next.js
- Database: PostgreSQL
- AI/ML: scikit-learn, XGBoost, TensorFlow/PyTorch for ML models; recommendation engine; time-series forecasting
- Hosting: Cloud deployment (TBD)

## Constraints

- Must demonstrate ML capabilities as part of SMU MBA Machine Learning course deliverable
- Singapore market focus — local F&B regulations and consumer behavior
- Must work as a real marketplace with two-sided user flows (merchants and consumers)
- Budget-conscious — leverage free tiers and open-source tools where possible

## Users

- **Merchant**: F&B business owner or manager who lists surplus food items, sets pricing, views waste analytics, and receives demand predictions
- **Consumer**: Budget-conscious user who browses discounted food deals, receives personalized recommendations, and places orders for pickup
- **Admin**: Platform operator who manages merchant onboarding, monitors marketplace health, and oversees ML model performance
